About NaijaTrainings

Connecting Nigerians to quality skill-based training programs across the nation

Our Story

NaijaTrainings was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality skills training accessible to every Nigerian, regardless of location or background.

We recognized that many Nigerians struggle to find legitimate, verified training programs in their areas. At the same time, quality training providers often lack the visibility to reach eager students. NaijaTrainings bridges this gap.

Today, we're proud to be Nigeria's leading platform connecting students with verified training providers across all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerians with access to quality skills training programs, helping them build successful careers and businesses. We strive to create a transparent marketplace where students can confidently choose from verified training providers.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for skills training in Nigeria, creating a future where every Nigerian has easy access to the training they need to succeed in their chosen field, whether online or in-person.
